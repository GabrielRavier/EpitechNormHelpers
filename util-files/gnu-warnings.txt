g5:
- -Wmissing-declarations
- -Wmissing-variable-declarations (clang-specific)

f5:

- -Wmissing-prototypes (clang-specific, doesn't do *exactly* what -Wstrict-prototypes does but it's close enough)

command line for all (GCC):
- -Wmissing-declarations -Wstrict-prototypes

command line for all (Clang):
- -Wmissing-declarations -Wmissing-variable-declarations -Wmissing-prototypes
